About Us

We are leading the Digital transformation from a largely bespoke, homegrown MACH-based commerce solution to a cloud-based, scalable, and agile technology landscape, which enables our clients to connect to their customers seamlessly across all channels in an increasingly digital and personalized way.
From rolling out eCommerce sites, for many of our customer's brands to migrating them towards a headless commerce architecture. Designing, building, and hosting mobile apps to make the customer journey easy. Delivering omnichannel capabilities which take all friction out of the customer experience. Building up a platform engineering capability that helps our clients connect to their customers faster and better. Delivering effective CRM, loyalty, and mobile order and pay solutions.
While executing the digital transformation we continue to support our client's live operations through ongoing, incremental improvements in our existing landscape, in all areas (applications, integration, networks, hosting, workplace).
Ensuring that we have continued, strong business and IT alignment, both through structured program and service management processes as well as strong informal relationship building across all levels of the organization.
Taking the existing IT organization by hand throughout this change through ongoing communication, training, and development as well as targeted injection of fresh talent from outside.
